## Problem

Some service variants, such as nail services, benefit from visual examples or samples that help customers choose.

## Smallest Useful Version

Allow salons to attach sample images to a `ServiceVariant` and show them on public service views.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A service variant can have sample images.
- [ ] Manager can add, remove, and reorder samples.
- [ ] Public salon page can show samples where available.
- [ ] Missing samples do not affect booking flow.
