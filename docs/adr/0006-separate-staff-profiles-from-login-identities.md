# Separate Staff Profiles from login identities

A salon owns its Staff Profiles and operational history, while each staff member owns their verified login identity: a phone-bound Staff Invite accepted by the invited person creates revocable Staff Profile Access, and one identity may hold access in multiple salons without managers creating credentials. Revocation or self-leave ends only that permission link and preserves salon-owned records; we rejected admin-controlled identity transfer and the old single-salon identity limit because both confuse operational ownership with authentication identity and do not match multi-salon staff work.
