# Pending AppointmentRequests do not hold availability

A pending `AppointmentRequest` does not reserve its requested slot. Multiple customers can submit requests for the same slot in parallel, and the only thing preventing a double-book is the manager picking one to approve (which triggers `Appointment Intake` and 409s any subsequent approval). We considered a soft-hold (slot reserved for N minutes/hours after submit) and rejected it: the real throttle we want is **deposits/prepayment**, which is on the near-term roadmap and is what should gate a slot. Adding soft-hold now means building expiry jobs, staff-vs-public hold visibility, and "hold released" UX that would all be torn out the moment deposits land. Until then, the customer confirmation copy is explicit ("request submitted, salon will contact you" + salon phone) so no customer is misled into thinking their slot is reserved. The schema columns `paymentStatus` and `depositAmount` on `appointment_requests`, and `depositPolicy` on `salon_public_settings`, exist now so the deposit flow can be wired in without a breaking change.
